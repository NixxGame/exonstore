Survivor Distance

Blind Progress

Killer position revealed through walls with a distinct aura color.

Killer name tag always visible regardless of walls or distance.

Killer Distance

Live distance counter to the killer updated every frame.

Killer Character

Displays the killer's character name so you know exactly what power you're up against.

Killer Compass

Compass indicator for the killer showing approach direction at a glance.

Crosshair

Toggleable crosshair overlay for consistent aim reference on the killer side.

Generator Labels & Auras

All generators shown with both a text label and an aura — track repair progress at a glance.

Chest Labels & Auras

Every chest on the map highlighted with a label and colored aura, opened or not.

Pallet Labels & Auras
